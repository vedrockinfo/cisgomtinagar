Our Programmes

We believe that curriculum plays a vital role in every pre and primary school activity, right from interactions and meals to singing and playing. We never miss an opportunity to educate. We have the best-in-class curriculum designed in-house using a combination of Playway and Montessori methods.

PLAYGROUP (Joyful B)

Every day is filled with fun activities and everything we do is designed to educate.

LOWER KG (Joyful C)

As children get older, our comprehensive Lower KG programme helps them explore and communicate in class settings.

UPPER KG (Joyful D)

Nursery children at City International School Juniors prepare for a lifetime of learning in a nurturing environment

Class 1 to 5

As children become increasingly independent, they aspire for new challenges for which we prepare them.

who we are?

City International School promotes a broader and bolder education. The process of learning incorporates the 21st Century 4C+4C Skills.
