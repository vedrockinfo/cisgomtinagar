Our Vision & Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is deeply rooted in the Montessori philosophy, creating a nurturing environment where children, spanning from Playgroup to Grade 5, embark on a transformative journey of self-discovery and growth. We emphasize the cultivation of a mindset that encourages personal excellence, resilience, and an insatiable curiosity for learning.

At the core of our educational approach is a tailored learning experience that recognizes and respects the unique strengths and abilities of each child. We believe in fostering a supportive community that not only facilitates academic achievement but also nurtures the development of essential life skills.

Through this holistic approach, we aim to empower students to compete with themselves, fostering a deep-seated commitment to personal growth. By equipping every child with the necessary skills, unwavering confidence, and a genuine love for learning, we aspire to help them surpass their own expectations.

Ultimately, our mission extends beyond academic success; we are dedicated to preparing our students for a future where they can confidently navigate challenges, demonstrate resilience in the face of adversity, and contribute meaningfully to the world around them. In our Montessori-based school, we believe that by embracing this vision and mission, we are laying the foundation for a lifetime of success, fulfillment, and positive impact.

Our Vision

In our Montessori-based school, our vision is centered on the profound concept of "Cultivating Self-Driven Excellence: Empowering Each Child to Surpass Their Personal Best." This aspirational statement encapsulates our commitment to instilling a sense of self-motivation and continuous improvement in every child under our care.

Our Moto and Philosophy

Our mission is to lead culturally diverse and family-oriented international school, to benefit from the diversity of its community in creating well-educated, internationally minded citizens.

We inspire young individuals to develop their intellect, creativity and character to grow into adaptable, socially responsible global citizens by ensuring a dynamic education of high standard.

We believe that:

  • Honesty and integrity are essential for building trusting relationships.
  • Each person is unique and has equal worth
  • All people are responsible for their actions.
  • Open-mindedness, empathy and compassion are essential for living in a global community.
  • A thorough education includes the development of the qualities of compassion, tolerance, respect for the rights and cultures of all people, the skills for the peaceful resolution of conflict, and the development of environmental responsibility.
  • Developing head, heart and hands is necessary to realize one’s potential
  • Expectations that are challenging lead to higher levels of performance and improve results.
  • Commitment, enthusiasm and perseverance lead to success.
  • Education is a shared responsibility of students, family and school.
  • Everyone has the responsibility to contribute positively to society and the environment.
  • Celebrating diversity enriches our lives and the life of the community.
  • Nurturing curiosity and creativity stimulates exploration and discovery throughout life.
  • School should foster an atmosphere of academic excellence and encourage intellectual inquiry and critical thinking.
  • Achievement in the physical, expressive and creative arts is an essential part of a holistic education.
  • The School is a community and social awareness, thoughtful interaction and effective communication among students, parents and staff are fundamental to its well-being.

Our Director's Message

Thank you for considering City International School Juniors for your child’s learning, becoming and growing.

As the director of the School, I am pleased to lead such a dynamic and diverse organization.

Our school believes in educating the “whole child”. We adhere to the philosophies of education and curricula that are concept-driven, when students are guided in constructing meaning through making connections across curriculum areas and with the real world. We also believe that teachers have a very important role to play in inspiring students and staff to become life-long learners.

We hold the loftiest of aspirations for our school and a singleness of purpose that we will do all possible to prepare our students to achieve to the highest level possible, to lead, to be honorable, ethical and adaptable. Happily, the reflections of our children and their parents speak of the quality of relationships between teachers and students. Our students are challenged by their teachers and this community to be their best and to care for one another.

City International School Juniors is a very young dynamic school. Our reputation is founded on the motivation of our students, the skill of our faculty and unprecedented participation by our parent community.

We hold uncommonly high expectations and our student learning results provide every reason to be proud.

We cordially invite you to visit our school and view all the wonderful activities that occur on a daily basis.

Sincerely
Director

Our Principal's message

It is with great pleasure and enthusiasm that I extend a warm welcome to all parents, students and visitors to our school, where we embrace the Montessori method of teaching.

At CIS Juniors, we believe in creating an educational environment that is as unique as each child it serves. The Montessori approach, championed by Maria Montessori, is woven into the fabric of our institution, shaping the way we nurture and educate our students.

Our classrooms are vibrant spaces where curiosity is sparked, independence is fostered, and a love for learning is ignited. The Montessori method recognizes the individuality of each child, catering to diverse learning styles and fostering a sense of ownership over one's education.

Our dedicated team of educators is passionate about guiding students through a journey of self-discovery, emphasizing not only academic excellence but also the development of social and emotional intelligence. Through hands-on learning experiences and collaborative projects, we aim to cultivate well-rounded individuals poised for success in the ever-changing landscape of the future.

As you explore our school website, you'll discover the richness of our Montessori-inspired curriculum, the dedication of our faculty, and the multitude of opportunities available to our students. We invite you to join us in creating an educational experience that goes beyond traditional boundaries, where learning is an adventure and every child is celebrated. Thank you for choosing our school as the foundation for your child's educational journey. We look forward to fostering a love for learning that will last a lifetime.

Warm regards,
Principal
